72-hour study plan

SatApr 25
Day 1 — Build the framework. Work through the Main Study Guide theme by theme. ~30 min per theme, take a break between each. Open the image gallery alongside for visual reinforcement. Don't worry about memorization yet — focus on understanding.
SunApr 26
Day 2 — Drill cold. Take the Active Recall drill without notes — score yourself. Anything below 80% on a theme = priority review. Re-read those chunks in the study guide. Then re-take the drill (use "Reset everything" in sidebar). Also work through the Practice Questions PDF — these mirror the actual exam format.
Mon AMApr 27
Exam day — Final scan. Re-read the "Top 25 must-knows" at the top of the study guide. Skim any matched-area images you got wrong. Don't try to learn anything new — trust the work you've done.
